What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?

+
GEO is the practice of making a brand citable by AI engines like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity and Google AI Overviews. Where SEO optimizes pages to rank as links on a results page, GEO optimizes the same kind of content so language models can pull a sentence out of it and attribute it back to you. In practice it combines three things: writing that contains real, quotable answers; markup the model can parse; and the credibility signals — credentials, mentions, consistency — that make an engine trust you in the first place.

How is GEO different from SEO?

+
SEO is about being clickable. GEO is about being quotable. They share the same foundation — clear, well-structured, credible content — but GEO adds the one thing SEO doesn't measure: whether a model is willing to put your name in the answer it gives.

What does a brand reputation audit actually look like?

+
It reviews how your brand reads today — through reviews, sentiment, mentions, and the answers AI engines give when someone asks about you. noelia.biz delivers a free version in under 24 hours, in English, French or Spanish, structured as a one-page diagnostic and three things you can act on this week.

How do I know if AI engines are citing my brand?

+
Ask each engine — ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini — the questions your buyers would ask, in the languages your buyers speak, and log whether your brand appears and where. Repeat monthly. That share-of-AI-voice score is the metric I watch most, and it's exactly what the free 24-hour audit measures for you.

AI vs marketing automation — what's the actual difference?

+
Automation runs the rules you wrote (if email opened, send follow-up). AI generates outputs you didn't pre-write (write a follow-up tailored to this lead's last reply). Most teams need both: automation for the plumbing, AI for the judgment calls. Mistaking one for the other is the fastest way to spend money on AI to solve a problem automation already solved.
